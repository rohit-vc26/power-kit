# Follow-Up Email Template

**Use 3 days after first email (no reply).**

---

## Subject Lines (Pick One)

- `Re: [Original subject]`
- `[Name], quick question`
- `Did this slip through?`
- `Following up`

---

## Email Body (65-90 words)

```
Hi [Name],

I sent you [original email topic] a few days ago. Just checking it didn't slip through.

If you're not interested, no problem. Reply with "no thanks" and I'll stop.

If you are, here's the short version:
- [Key benefit 1]
- [Key benefit 2]

→ [CTA to action]

P.S. Other [audience type] saw [specific result]. Worth 5 minutes.
```

---

## Check Before Sending

- [ ] Sounds human (conversational)
- [ ] No em-dashes
- [ ] 65-90 words
- [ ] Clear out (reply "no thanks")
- [ ] CTA + hook
- [ ] No spam words
- [ ] Real benefits only
