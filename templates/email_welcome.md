# Welcome Email Template

**Use this as a starting point. Customize for your campaign.**

---

## Subject Lines (Pick One)

- `Welcome to [Company]`
- `[Name], welcome — here's what to do next`
- `Your account is ready`
- `Quick start guide inside`

---

## Email Body (65-90 words)

```
Hi [Name],

Welcome to [Company]. You've joined [number] users who [achievement].

Here's what's next:

1. [Action step 1]
2. [Action step 2]  
3. [Action step 3]

Have questions? Reply to this email. A real person reads every one.

→ [Get started CTA button]

P.S. Most users finish setup in under 5 minutes. We're here if you get stuck.
```

---

## Check Before Sending

- [ ] Sounds human (not AI)
- [ ] No em-dashes
- [ ] 65-90 words
- [ ] CTA + hook present
- [ ] No spam words
- [ ] No fake promises
- [ ] No guarantees
- [ ] Real "P.S." (not made up)
